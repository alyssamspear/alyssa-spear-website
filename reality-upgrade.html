Reality Upgrade Brand Identity

As the Founder and Creative Director of Reality Upgrade, a community-driven nonprofit and brand project, I directed all aspects of daily operations, marketing strategies, product designs, and creative output. We allocated 60% of profits to community projects based on stakeholder voting models.

This project was a massive brand development exercise. I designed our entire visual identity system including custom logos, typography rules, web banners, and printed labels. I created design layouts for retail products, apparel, and packaging. I also illustrated and animated a complete children's book and companion short film in Adobe Illustrator and After Effects.
