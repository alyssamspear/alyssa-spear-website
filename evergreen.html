Product Line Expansion

At Evergreen Research & Marketing, I served as a Graphic Designer responsible for transforming individual product concepts into comprehensive, cohesive product line packaging systems. My role focused on creating visual harmony across diverse layouts and ensuring technical compliance.

I designed dynamic graphics and motion assets for client deliverables using Adobe Illustrator and After Effects. I led client presentations, integrated stakeholder feedback, and delivered print-ready production files. Additionally, I art-directed photography and video shoots to align with broader marketing campaign objectives.
