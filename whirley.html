Product to Shelf: 7-Eleven Nationwide

During my tenure at Whirley DrinkWorks, I designed a 16 oz coffee mug that was selected from multiple creative concepts and distributed to 7-Eleven stores nationwide. This project required strict adherence to corporate brand guidelines, detailed formatting of print-ready dielines for volume production, and close collaboration with manufacturing teams.

In addition to product styling, I took charge of planning, organizing, and editing over 1,000 product images for the corporate website, ensuring clean backgrounds and accurate color representation. I also developed graphic materials for national trade show displays, product catalogs, and promotional client decks.
